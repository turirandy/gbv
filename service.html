Our services

Fighting Sgbv through Education,Support and Action

What We Offer

Our services are centered around empowering communities,supporting survivors,and eliminating the root causes of sexual and gender-based violence.

Community Education

We conduct workshops,forums and school outreach programs to educate people about gender-based violence,consent,heathy relationships and human rights.

Survivor Support

We connect survivors with professional counselors,legal advisors,safe houses and medical support

Training for Advocates

We train volunteers,teachers and youth leaders to become community champions in identifying,reporting and addressing SGBV cases effectively and compassionately

Ending the Cycle of Silence

Our campaigns use media,art and technology to challenge cultural norms that fuel silence around abuse.

We encourage survivors to share their stories and take part in healing initiatives
